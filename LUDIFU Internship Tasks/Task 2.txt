SEO stands for Search Engine Optimisation. 
It means the process of improving your site to increase its visibility when people search for products or services related to your business in Google, Bing, and other search engines. 

Why is SEO important for marketing....?
SEO is a fundamental part of digital marketing because people conduct innumerable of searches every year, often with commercial intent to find information about products and services.
