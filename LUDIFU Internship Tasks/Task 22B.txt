Useful email phrases:

1. Thank you for your quick reply. 
2. Any assistance could you give me in this matter will be greatly appreciated. 
3. I apologize but I am afraid we are going to have to reschedule. 
4. Thank you so much for your patience while we sort this things out. 
5. Can you please do it at your earliest..? 
6. Use passive voice. Ex. Mistake was made. 
7. I m looking for hearing from you soon!
