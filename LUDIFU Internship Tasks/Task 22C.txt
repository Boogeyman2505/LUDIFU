*Task 22C*

Phrases for formal email - business english...!!

Greetings -

1) Always write Ms. and not Mrs. when writing to a woman. 
2) Do not write there name instead write surname. 
Ex. Ms. Franklin 

Reason for writing mail -
Begin with.. 
1) I am writing in connection with.. 
2) Writing with regards to.. 
3) Write about attachment that you have attached. 

Be formal enough and write point to point mail.
