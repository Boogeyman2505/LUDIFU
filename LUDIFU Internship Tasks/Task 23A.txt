Speak English Fluently - The 5 Steps To Improve Your English Fluency

Step 1: Don't Give A Damn. Speak Whenever, Wherever, However. Just Speaks

Step 2: Read Out Loud. Read a book word by word, paragraph by paragraph.

Step 3: Learn One New Word Everyday that we dont understand, never heard before.

Step 4: Immerse Yourself. Listen English Songs. Watch English Movies without subtitles.

Step 5: Spend Time With People Who Speak English

Step 6: Go Into a State of Over Learning
